Abstract

Animal-worn sensors have revolutionised ecology, but classification remains challenging due to class imbalance and unquantified uncertainty. We introduce an open-source approach using 1D CNNs and conformal prediction (RAPS) to classify behaviour with statistically valid uncertainty sets.
